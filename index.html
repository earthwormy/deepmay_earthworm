Free grocery giveaways

Five weekly locations around Atlanta.
Come get food, help out, or both!
Just show up:

  • Edgewood: Every Sunday 1-3:00, corner of Mayson and Hardee
  • Clarkston: Every Tuesday 4:30-6, 4540 E Ponce de Leon Ave
  • East Atlanta: Every Wednesday 6:00, ICP trailhead park parking lot
  • Westlake: Every Thursday 2-3:30, 1616 Joseph E Boone
